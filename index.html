The Diagnosis

It's not a content problem. It's an engineering problem.

Most fitness creators and studios post great training content that dies in the feed. Not because it's bad — because pacing, hooks and structure leak viewers at predictable points. Bad pacing is the silent killer of great content: your audience feels it, the algorithm punishes it.

LEAK 01

The dead hook

Your first 3 seconds answer no question and promise no payoff — viewers swipe before your value even starts.

LEAK 02

The mid-roll drop

One pacing dip in the middle — a slow explanation, a dead frame — and 40% of the audience silently leaves.

LEAK 03

The pointless ending

No loop, no CTA, no reason to rewatch or follow. The video ends and so does the relationship.
